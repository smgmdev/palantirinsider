Section 01

The Core Insight — Data Is Everywhere Already

Every government and military already generates enormous amounts of data. The problem is it's siloed, un-analysed, and invisible. That's the gap — not missing satellites.

Palantir's founding insight (2003): The US intelligence community already had all the data it needed to prevent 9/11. The failure was not a lack of collection — it was a lack of connection. The CIA had pieces. The FBI had pieces. They sat in different databases that never talked to each other. Palantir was built to connect those databases and find the patterns. Satellites had nothing to do with it.

✅ What You Can Do Without Satellites

  • Fuse drone feeds, radio intercepts, human reports into one picture
  • Track logistics — ammunition, fuel, medical supplies in real time
  • Detect financial crime across millions of transactions
  • Connect hospital databases across an entire country
  • Build a government operations platform for 20+ ministries
  • AI-assisted targeting from drone video alone
  • Cyber threat intelligence and network monitoring
  • Border control — ground sensors + camera feeds + databases
  • Counter-terrorism — phone records, financial flows, travel data
  • Infrastructure monitoring — power grid, water, telecoms

🛰️ What Requires Satellites (Add Later)

  • Wide-area change detection — spotting new military buildup
  • Open-ocean vessel tracking
  • Monitoring denied areas you can't fly drones into
  • Global persistent surveillance of multiple regions
  • Verifying treaty compliance from space
  • Disaster damage assessment at scale
