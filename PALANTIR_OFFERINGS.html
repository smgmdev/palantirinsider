Section 01
4 Core Platforms

Everything Palantir sells runs on one of four platforms. Each targets a different part of the stack — data integration, AI, decision-making, or deployment. Clients rarely buy just one.

Artificial Intelligence
AIP
Artificial Intelligence Platform — AI on your private network, under full control

Deploys LLMs and AI models inside a client's secure environment. The AI reasons on live enterprise data without sending anything to the cloud. Ranked #1 in AI/ML by Dresner Advisory, IDC, and Forrester (2024).

  • AIP Logic — author LLM-driven functions, no coding required
  • AIP Scenarios — simulate AI decisions before executing them
  • AIP Automate — AI-triggered automations on new data/events
  • AIP Tool Factory — converts data & models into AI-usable tools
  • Vector Management — embeddings & semantic search via Ontology
  • AIP Bootcamps — zero to production in days, not months
  • Granular AI Guardrails — role, classification, purpose-based access
Enterprise Operating System
Foundry
The Ontology-Powered Operating System for the Modern Enterprise

Connects every data source, model, and team inside a company into one unified operating layer. The Ontology maps every object, action, and process in real time — enabling AI workflows across the entire organisation.

  • Data Integration — multi-source connectors, no duplication
  • Pipeline Builder — code-free production data pipelines
  • Digital Twin — virtual system representation for simulation
  • Process Mining — analyse & optimise workflows automatically
  • Dynamic Scheduling — AI resource allocation in real time
  • Streaming — real-time data processing layer
  • Real-Time Alerting — automated notifications from live data
  • Marketplace — share and reuse ontology components across teams
Defence & Intelligence
Gotham
The Operating System for Global Decision Making

The original Palantir product. Fuses massive volumes of near-real-time data from thousands of sensors into a single operational picture. Used by defence agencies and intelligence communities for over a decade. From HQ to forward-deployed edge.

  • Single pane of glass across millions of sensors
  • Operational AI with live feedback loops — models improve during ops
  • Certified: FedRAMP, IL-5 DoD SRG, SOC 2, ISO 27001
  • Anti-lock-in — all data exportable with provenance
  • Edge deployment — works offline in forward positions
  • Multi-domain: land, air, sea, space, cyber integration
Deployment Infrastructure
Apollo
The Operating System for Continuous Delivery — anywhere, any environment

The engine that runs Palantir's other platforms everywhere — cloud, on-prem, airgapped networks, ruggedised edge devices, submarines. Moves from manual deployment to fully autonomous with zero human intervention required.

  • Software Control Center — full visibility across all deployments
  • Autonomous continuous deployment — self-managing releases
  • Multi-cloud: AWS, Azure, GCP, private cloud, hybrid
  • Airgapped & high-side network support
  • SecOps — encode and enforce security policies at scale
  • Day 2 Operations — rule-based compliance and reliability

AIP — AI Platform Deep Dive

AIP is Palantir's fastest-growing product and the centrepiece of their commercial expansion. It sits on top of Foundry and brings AI into live operational decision-making — without exposing private data to third-party AI providers.

3-Layer Architecture

LayerNameWhat It Does
Layer 1Semantic Data LayerIntegrates structured, unstructured, streaming, and geospatial data into a secure real-time multi-modal foundation. No raw data leaves the environment.
Layer 2Dynamic Logic LayerAnchors AI reasoning in deterministic calculations, business rules, ML models, and optimisers. AI can't hallucinate past the logic guardrails.
Layer 3Kinetic Action LayerSyncs AI decisions back to ERPs, MES, SCMs, scheduling platforms, and edge systems. The AI doesn't just advise — it executes.

AIP Core Products

Authoring
AIP Logic
Build, manage, and deploy LLM-driven functions with full testing and debugging. No coding required. Functions usable by humans and AI agents interchangeably.
Safety
AIP Scenarios
Simulate and examine AI-driven decisions and their downstream consequences before committing to action. Full data introspection at every step.
Automation
AIP Automate
Define AI-driven automations that trigger within specific, scoped boundaries when new data arrives or conditions change. Human-in-the-loop optional.
AI Infrastructure
AIP Tool Factory
Converts existing data pipelines, models, and enterprise processes into AI-tuned tools — callable by both human analysts and autonomous AI agents.
Search & Retrieval
Vector Management
Generates embeddings, manages vector stores, and powers semantic search across all enterprise data via the Palantir Ontology framework.
Onboarding
AIP Bootcamps
Intensive hands-on programmes that take client teams from zero to a live production AI use case in days. Not a demo — actual deployed software.
Why AIP matters for Project MAX: AIP is exactly what Project MAX needs to replicate — the AI layer that turns raw satellite data into actionable threat intelligence. The open-source equivalents (PyTorch + TensorRT + LangChain + custom ontology) can replicate 80% of this at a fraction of the cost.

US Department of Defense

Satellite Intelligence
MetaConstellation
Palantir's satellite orchestration layer built on Gotham. Coordinates tasking across multiple commercial satellite operators (Planet, ICEYE, BlackSky, Satellogic, Maxar) through a single API.
